## Pressure of Fluids

`$$ P = \frac{F}{A} $$`

`$$ 1 Pa = 1 \frac{N}{m^2} $$`

## Pressure varies with depth

`$$ P = P_0 + \rho g h $$`

where "P knot" is atmospheric pressure

`$$ P_0 = 1 atm = 1.013 \times 10^5 Pa $$`
