### Precambrian Eons

4.57 BYA - 538 MYA

**4.57 BYA** is the reckoned age of the Earth, determined from the oldest **meteorites** found.

Subdivided by Hadean, Archean, and Proterozoic Eons

---

### Hadean Eon

4.57 BYA - 4.03 BYA

- The word "Hadean" comes from "Hades" because the condition on Earth was pretty hellish.
- Initial atmosphere of water vapor, methane, ammonia.

### Hadean Eon

4.57 BYA - 4.03 BYA

- Eventually, the water vapor cooled into a superocean.
- Lots of volcanoes and asteroid bombardments. More nitrogen and CO2 released to the atmosphere.

### Archean Eon

4.03 BYA - 2.50 BYA

- The earliest known life, mostly represented by shallow-water microbial mats called stromatolites, started in the Archean Eon
- By the end of the era, cyanobacteria began creating oxygen.
- Photosynthesis!
