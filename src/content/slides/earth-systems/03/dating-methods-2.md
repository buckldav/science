### What is a half-life?

- The amount of time it takes for half of the parent isotopes to decay to daughter isotopes.

<img src="/assets/slides/halflife.jpg" style="margin: 0 auto; display: block; height: 60vh">

---

#### How can beta decay and half-lives tell us the age of a material?

- The percentage of undecayed material and the half-life can tell us how old something is. <!-- .element: class="fragment" data-fragment-index="1" -->
- If 50% of the material remains, the substance is one half-life old. <!-- .element: class="fragment" data-fragment-index="2" -->
- If 25% of the material remains, the substance is two half-lives old. <!-- .element: class="fragment" data-fragment-index="3" -->
- etc. <!-- .element: class="fragment" data-fragment-index="3" -->
