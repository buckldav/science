#### Continental Drift

<style>
li { font-size: 2rem;  }
</style>

- Theorized by Alfred Wegener in 1912. <!-- .element: class="fragment" data-fragment-index="1" -->
- Obscure until Plate Tectonics Theory popularized in the 60s. <!-- .element: class="fragment" data-fragment-index="2" -->
- Mantle Convection moves Tectonic Plates which causes Continental Drift. <!-- .element: class="fragment" data-fragment-index="3" -->
- Example: North American Plate is moving 1 inch away from Europe yearly. <!-- .element: class="fragment" data-fragment-index="4" -->
