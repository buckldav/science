#### Define Weather and Climate

- **Weather** refers to the **short-term** atmospheric conditions at a specific time and place.
- **Climate** describes the **long-term** average of those conditions over a period of typically 30 years. 
- Essentially, weather is what you experience daily, and climate is what you expect based on historical patterns.
